How to Play

  • 🎯 Goal: Arrange tiles in order (1…N), blank at bottom-right.
  • 👆 Click/Tap any tile next to the blank to slide it.
  • ⌨️ Use Arrow Keys to slide tiles.
  • 📱 On mobile, swipe on the board.
  • 🔀 New Game shuffles (always solvable!).
  • ⏱ Timer starts on your first move.
  • 🏆 Beat your Best time per difficulty!
